The safe street journal

An exclusively online media that promotes debate and presents the world's most burning issues.

The safe street journal is a new media, which aim is to foster debate and allow everyone to express itself. Our ultimate goal is to assist every single one of our users to grasp the complexity of each subject. The aim of the start-up is to recruit journalists that want to protect the debate area and help every citizen to have access to verified, balanced and high-quality information. The purpose of The safe street journal, by helping its users and citizens, to help to protect democracy.
